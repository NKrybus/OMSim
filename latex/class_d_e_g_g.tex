\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
The \mbox{\hyperlink{class_d_e_g_g}{DEGG}} class is responsible for constructing the D-\/\+Egg detector geometry. It creates the pressure vessel, the inner volume, and places the PMTs inside the logical volume of the gel.
