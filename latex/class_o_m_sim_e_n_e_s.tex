{\footnotesize\ttfamily void OMSim\+ENES\+::\+Generate\+Primaries (\begin{DoxyParamCaption}\item[{G4\+Event $\ast$}]{an\+Event }\end{DoxyParamCaption})}

This function sets the initial properties of the electronic neutrinos such as position, and energy for the primary event based on given input parameters or data files. The energy is determined from a tabulated distribution or from a fixed mean energy, and the neutrino\textquotesingle{}s incoming direction is assumed to be (0,0,-\/1). The function also computes the angle distribution and the electron\textquotesingle{}s energy derived from the neutrino energy and its direction. Finally, the function computes the total cross-\/section and sets weights accordingly, passing the information to the analysis manager.
